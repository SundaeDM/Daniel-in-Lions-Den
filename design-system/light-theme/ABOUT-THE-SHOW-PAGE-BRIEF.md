# About the Show Page Brief — Light / Warm Storybook Theme

---

## Page Purpose

Same as dark theme: convert a curious parent into a confident buyer by answering "Is this worth it? Is it right for my kids?" This page is the second most important sales page after the homepage.

---

## How This Differs from the Dark Theme Version

The dark theme About page builds **anticipation** — tension, spectacle, revelation. This version builds **affection**. The parent should feel like they're being introduced to old friends: Daniel is brave and kind, the lions are majestic but gentle, Esther is bold and good. By the end of this page, they're not just buying a ticket — they're investing in characters their children will love.

---

## Visual Brightness

**Predominantly light — warmest version of the site.** Dark only at the footer. The hero is the only near-dark section (warm brown, not black). Everything else is cream, parchment, or the sage green section.

Section rhythm: Warm dark hero → Cream story → Parchment Esther → Cream characters → Parchment specs → Cream gallery → Gold CTA → Warm dark footer

---

## Section Inventory

### 1. PAGE HERO
- **Background:** Warm brown (`#2C1A08`) with show key art at reduced opacity as a warm texture — not a full bleed dramatic photo as in the dark theme
- **Content:** Page title "About the Show" in Playfair Display, `--color-gold-bright`. Subhead: "A story of courage. A show for the whole family." Nunito, cream.
- **Height:** 50vh desktop — more generous than the schedule page, less full than the homepage hero
- **Decorative:** Scroll flourish motif below the subtitle

### 2. THE STORY OF DANIEL
- **Background:** Cream (`#FDF5E6`)
- **Layout:** Centered, single-column, max-width 700px
- **Headline:** "A Boy, a King, and Three Very Surprising Lions." (Playfair Display, amber — note the warmth and wonder in this headline vs. the dark theme's "A Story of Courage That Changed the World")
- **Decorative rule:** Small `◆ ─── ◆` gold motif below the headline
- **Body copy (3 paragraphs):**
  - Para 1: Daniel's world — painted with warmth and wonder. He's a young man who believes good things. He's taken to a strange place. He's asked to give up what he loves most.
  - Para 2: The night in the lions' den — not terrifying but miraculous. The lions lie down. Morning comes. The king runs.
  - Para 3: What your family will experience together — the show as an adventure you'll share, not a lecture you'll sit through.
- **Pull quote:** Playfair italic, large (30px), amber, centered, with thin amber rules above and below. Example: *"He trusted, and the lions were still."*

### 3. ALSO FEATURING: ESTHER
- **Background:** Warm blush parchment (`#F9EBF0` — a very soft rose-tinted cream, distinct from the main cream but still light)
- **Layout:** Two-column: left = Esther character art in a soft rose-framed oval, right = copy
- **Headline:** "Also Featuring Esther — The Bravest Queen" in Playfair Display, `--color-esther-rose`
- **Decorative rule:** Small vine/leaf motif in `--color-esther-rose` below the headline
- **Body copy (2 paragraphs):** Esther's story told with warmth and wonder — emphasize her courage as a choice, her grace, her impact. She's not a minor feature; she's a co-star.
- **Tone:** Especially resonant for parents of daughters — make Esther feel as compelling as Daniel

### 4. MEET THE CHARACTERS
- **Background:** Cream (`#FDF5E6`)
- **Headline:** "Meet the Characters" — Playfair Display, amber
- **Layout:** Horizontal scroll of illustrated character cards (4 cards)
- **Each card:**
  - Character illustration (warm-toned, portrait crop)
  - Gold oval frame around portrait
  - Name in Playfair Display, amber
  - Role descriptor in Nunito, 13px, `--color-text-muted` (e.g., "Faithful and Brave")
  - 2-sentence description in Nunito, `--color-text-dark`
  - Card fill: `--color-bg-sand`. Border: 1px `--color-gold-accent`. Radius: 12px (rounder than show cards — characters feel friendly). Shadow: warm.
- **Characters:** Daniel, One of the Lions (named, given personality), Esther, King Darius

### 5. WHAT TO EXPECT
- **Background:** Warm parchment (`#F5EDD8`)
- **Headline:** "What to Expect" — Playfair Display, amber
- **Layout:** 4-tile grid. Each tile: illustrated icon (warm SVG style), label, value, short description
  1. 🎭 Show Length — 90 minutes. No intermission.
  2. 👏 Audience Participation — Yes! Bring your energy.
  3. 👨‍👩‍👧 Recommended Ages — 3 and up.
  4. ✨ Special Effects — Live music, dramatic lighting, and stage magic.
- **Tile style:** Cream fill (`#FDF5E6`), 1px `--color-gold-accent` border, 8px radius, warm shadow. Icon in amber/gold.
- **Sensory note:** Below the grid in small Nunito, `--color-text-muted`: "Sensory note: The show includes live music, dramatic lighting, and some loud moments. Ear protection is welcome for sensory-sensitive guests."

### 6. "WHY FAMILIES LOVE IT" — TESTIMONIALS
- **Background:** Sage green (`#5E7A52`)
- **Content:** 3 parent quotes, Playfair italic, cream. Attribution: Nunito small, `--color-sage-soft`.
- **Decorative:** Small cream star (★) before each quote
- **Note:** This repeats from the homepage but is appropriate here — it's the emotional peak of the trust-building arc for a parent who went deeper into the site

### 7. GALLERY
- **Background:** Cream (`#FDF5E6`)
- **Headline:** "From the Stage" — Playfair Display, amber
- **Layout:** 3×2 grid desktop, 2×2 mobile. Warm-toned photography. Images have a soft 2px `--color-gold-accent` border and 8px radius.
- **Lightbox:** Cream background (not dark as in the dark theme). Gold close button and navigation arrows.
- **Hover:** Slight scale + warm brightness boost

### 8. BUY TICKETS CTA BLOCK
- **Background:** Gold amber (`#C8860A`) — the same celebratory gold used for the email signup
- **Content:** Show logo (light version). Tagline: "Ready to make a memory?" Large "BUY TICKETS" button (dark fill, cream text). Below: "Choose your city and find dates near you." in cream Nunito.
- **Decorative:** Small paw print motifs at low opacity as watermark behind the content
- **Height:** 320px minimum.

### 9. FOOTER
- Standard warm dark footer.

---

## Mobile Behavior

- Story copy: full-width, comfortable line length at 390px
- Character cards: horizontal scroll (snap scrolling), 1.5 cards visible at a time to hint at scroll
- Specs grid: 2×2 on mobile
- Gallery: 2×2, full-screen lightbox with swipe navigation
- CTA block: title scales to 28px, button full-width

---

## Forbidden

- No cast/performer bios or headshots
- No video autoplay
- No timeline or chapter-by-chapter story spoilers
- No bullet-point Bible synopsis
- No dark dramatic lighting effects — all imagery should read as warm and inviting
